Week 10

Reshaping Data

Oct 25

Readings

  • “Tidy Data” by Hadley Wickham.
    • Ignore all the R code that you see, as basically all of it has now been superseded by newer tidyverse functions.
    • This was Wickham’s early “tidy tools” manifesto; it provided guiding principles and a framework for developing the tidyverse.
